Graduate Research Assistant

I am a PhD student at Department of Computer Science, University of Arizona.
My advisor is Prof. Kate Isaacs. My research interest is Information Visualization.

Research Projects

I am working with Prof. Kate Isaacs on designing, implementing and evaluating the visual analytics system - CFGExplorer - to help compiler researchers with program analysis. CFGExplorer is a visual control flow analytics System designed around basic program analysis operations. https://github.com/hdc-arizona/cfgexplorer
